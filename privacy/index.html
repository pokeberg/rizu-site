Privacy Policy

Rizu is designed to be simple and respectful of your privacy.

Data collection: Rizu does not sell your data.

Analytics: (If you use none) Rizu does not use third-party analytics to track you across apps or websites.

Account: Rizu does not require you to create an account.

iCloud (if enabled): If you enable iCloud, your app data may sync via Apple’s iCloud services between your devices.

Contact: If you email support, we’ll receive your email address and the information you include so we can help you.

Changes: This policy may be updated. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
